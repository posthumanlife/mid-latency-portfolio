about

my occupation is unknown. not that it is a secret — it is merely because i don't really know what i am. what i do know is that our society is rigged in the way it's organized now. we're hoping for some techno-fixes for our climate, while what we actually do is look away and carry on with our lives. children are the new seeds of our society that should be supported in their free development of understanding, rationally and emotionally, but also in their movements and ways of thinking. we need to build a platform for cooperation, common the school system and think together with our kids about our future, the future of this planet and a post-growth society!
